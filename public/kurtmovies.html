The Best Movies According to Kurt

My top 3 movies of all time.

Olympus Has Fallen

The first in the "Has Fallen" Trilogy and a perfect introduction to the all-action style of the protagonist Mike Banning as he attempts to rescue President Benjamin Asher from a terorist attack inside the White House. I love this movie for its action scenes and in my opinion, well written plot.

London Has Fallen

The second installment in the series ramps up the action even more as Banning races to keep President Asher safe from an unrelenting terrorist attack in Central London. I love this movie because it's a great follow up to the first movie and the pace of this one never lets up!

Angel Has Fallen

The third installment in the series sees Banning pursued as the suspect in an assassination attempt on now President Alan Trumbull. This movie is a favourite of mine due to the plot portraying Banning as an anti-hero who must fight to clear his name and find who has set him up.
